TEAM 144

Colerain Rocks Robotics!

We are First Robotics Team 144. The Robotics Organization of Cardinals and Knights. We are based at Colerain High School Career Center in Cincinnati Ohio. Our team has been around since the mid-1990's in one form or another, starting life as the Walnut Hills/Proctor and Gamble team that won the national championship in 1994. After a few sponsorship changes and moves, we are once again competing in a FIRST robotics competition!
